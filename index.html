📈 Organic Search Trend Analysis Healthy Baseline
The site has maintained active organic visibility throughout the full report period.

www.yourbellplumbing.com generated 1,285 clicks and 495,874 total impressions from December 2025 through June 2026. March 2026 was the peak month. The main story is not a collapse — it is an opportunity to push solid mid-page-1 rankings into the top 3, which is where CTR jumps significantly.

What the Data Shows

Rankings That Are Working Right Now

  • "bell plumbing san mateo" — position 1.5: Near top of page 1 with 22% CTR. 72 clicks in the period from just 328 impressions. This is the site's best-performing query
  • "bell plumbing san carlos" — position 4.3: Strong brand presence in the home market. 78 clicks and 11.8% CTR. People are finding and clicking through
  • "shared sewer line responsibility" — position 3.0: A non-branded informational query ranking on page 1. 7 clicks, 3.7% CTR. Shows content is starting to earn rankings beyond brand terms
  • "plumbers san carlos" — position 11.5: Already on page 1 for this competitive local service term. Getting to top 5 here is achievable with targeted optimization
  • "san carlos plumber" — position 7.0: Page 1 ranking with 1,008 impressions/month. Only 0.4% CTR at position 7 — top 3 would deliver 10x more clicks

Why Impressions Dropped After March

  • March's 178K impression spike likely came from gaining temporary rankings on high-volume informational queries (trenchless sewer, water heater repair, etc.) that the site ranked for briefly before positions settled
  • This pattern is normal for newer sites building authority. Initial rankings are earned, tested by Google, then settled at a stable position — which is often lower than the initial spike
  • Average position on many queries drifted from the 17–22 range (Dec–Mar) toward the 25–35 range (Apr–Jun). Not a collapse — more like the positions finding their natural level
  • Clicks in June (178) are very close to December (112). The click floor has risen even as impressions normalized. This signals that CTR on ranked pages is improving
  • The priority now is strengthening E-E-A-T signals on core service pages so Google settles them at higher positions permanently
📊 The site is building organic momentum. March showed what is possible. The work now is stabilizing and growing from here.

Key moves: push "san carlos plumber" and "plumbers san carlos" from position 7–11 into top 3, add trust signals to service pages (license number, certifications, real reviews), and build more of the informational content that earned the March spike. The foundation is solid.
